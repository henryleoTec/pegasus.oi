Who we are

We the members of Graduates Association of Niger Delta [GAND] having solemnly, Unanimously and Firmly resolved to provide our selves a Constitution, Aware of the need to constitute ourselves into a united, Progressive and Developmental forum.

Our Vision

Working towards enlightening and empowering Niger- Delta Graduates, Building a patriotic and loyal society for the nation, Producing citizens to bring about positive change to the nation and world at large.

Our Mission

Bringing all Niger-Delta graduates under one umbrella after graduation from the university,to enable to create educational developed society in communication, connection and information sharing, that will assist in creating JOB opportunities
