Graduates Association of Niger Delta
[GAND] #Constitution......

We the members of Graduates Association of Niger Delta [GAND] having solemnly, Unanimously and Firmly resolved
to provide our selves a Constitution, Aware of the need to constitute ourselves into a united, Progressive and
Developmental forum, Determined to defined and defend our right, discharged our duties in promoting
development in an effort to strengthen development for the betterment of the society.
